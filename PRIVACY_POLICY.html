Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 July 2026

Sri Nidhi Study Hall provides school administration and student information features. Information entered by authorized users may include names, contact details, academic records, and related school records.

Data use

Data is used only to provide the app’s school administration and study hall functions. The app does not sell personal information.

Storage and security

Data may be stored by the web services configured by the app owner. Users should avoid entering information that is not necessary for school operations and should keep login credentials private.

Children

This app may contain student information entered by authorized school staff. The school or app administrator is responsible for obtaining any required permissions and using the data appropriately.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Sri Nidhi Study Hall administrator.
